Tribunals

Tribunals are specialized quasi-judicial bodies established by Acts of Parliament to efficiently and expertly resolve disputes in specific areas like tax, environment, competition, administrative law, etc. They offer quicker resolutions, expert decision-making, and greater accessibility compared to traditional courts. Importantly, Tribunal decisions are legally binding, and non-compliance can have serious legal consequences. Kenya Law's vast database of Tribunal decisions provides valuable insights into legal precedents and judicial reasoning.
